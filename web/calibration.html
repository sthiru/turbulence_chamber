Hot Plate 4D Calibration

Establishes 4D lookup table mapping hot plate temperature × fan speed → chamber temperature/Cn². Varies both hot plate temperature and fan speed in nested loops to generate comprehensive calibration data.

Warning: This is a long-running calibration. For 1 fan speed and 9 temperature steps (80-120°C), this could take ~2.4 hours. Additional fan speeds will multiply this duration.
Calibration Process:
  • Nested loops: fan speeds × hot plate temperatures
  • 4D lookup table will be generated (temperature × fan speed → chamber temp, Cn²)
  • Enables interpolation for any temperature/fan speed combination
  • Supports Cn² control system lookup
